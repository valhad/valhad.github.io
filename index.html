Activity

In the last 2 years I have designed and presented the following teacher development sessions:

  • “Using Coursebooks Creatively”, in which I looked at the ways teachers can engage students using their current coursebooks
  • “Speaking Activities for YLs”, where I focused on types of speaking activities and what children need to have to complete a speaking activity successfully
  • “Practising Grammar with YLs”, where I looked at what grammar is, how YLs learn grammar and how we can teach in a way that leads to effective learning
  • “Using Mobiles in the Classroom”, which I mainly based on Shaun Wilden’s ideas published in one of the issues of IH Journal and looked at how those ideas can be applied to our teaching context
  • “Drilling”, in which I focused on how to make traditional drilling more meaningful, purposeful and communicative
